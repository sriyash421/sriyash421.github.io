Research

I study human-robot interaction for systems that can be deployed in the real-world ensuring human safety and comfort. To demonstrate such systems, I want to work on real-world experiments with human subjects, collect user feedback and incorporate it to improve real-world deployment. Further, to complement this, I also work on algorithmic and theoretical improvements to learning algorithms in multi-agent and lifelong learning systems. I am also interested in multi-agent and lifelong learning.
