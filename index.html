I am a second year PhD student at the University of Washington advised by Abhishek Gupta and Natasha Jaques. I work at the intersection of reinforcement learning and robotics.

Before UW, I received my bachelors and masters from the Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur, where I worked with Prof. P P Chakrabarti on multi-agent reinforcement learning. Over the summers, I had the pleasure of working with Prof. Siddhartha Srinivasa at the Personal Robotics Lab, Prof. Sarath Chandar at Mila - Quebec AI Insitute, and the Media and Data Science Research Lab at Adobe.
